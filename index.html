My Project Concept

Frustrated with your China Internet? With the crackdown of VPNs in China, it is increasingly becoming hard to get access to internet beyond the great firewall of China, like Google, Facebook etc, so the use of encrypted internet traffic flowing through proxy servers is slowly emerging as a way to bypass such protocols in this place, so I would like to create a portable device that has a proxy server already installed where you can easily carry it with you and create a Wi-Fi hot spot for on the go, or you can connect power to its end keep it as a permanent setting in an office or home.


I am the sort of person that travels a lot and I always like to be connected to the Internet, my travels in China can somewhat be awkward when the VPN servers don’t work, whilst you remain in the country you have to deal with jumping over the great firewall of China. Recently I found this proxy server program called Shadowsocks, the program acts as a gateway between you and the internet to bypass local ISP’s (Internet service providers) who in turn block all traffic heading out to the websites mentioned earlier, integrating this software onto a device such as a Raspberry is a cheap and easily assembled solution to this problem., I believe that this sort of project will help me and others to develop the skillset needed in the future to secure an ideal job with the practice of JavaScript and programing.

I believe that any foreigner travelling to China would have a great advantage to have one of these devices in their possession, whether it would be for use at work or at home. Relating back to my first experience in China, because I didn’t have a working VPN, it was a very daunting memory being stuck in a foreign country without internet. I literary arrived at the Airport, found out that may cellular roaming wasn’t working, therefore, my Google translator wasn’t Working, as well Google maps and Google search engine, at that time it was like I was lost in the wilderness. From theses fond memories I would like to see a device capable of being a Proxy and a Wi-Fi router in one, as well as being able to be compact and portable to carry around in your handbag or man purse.
